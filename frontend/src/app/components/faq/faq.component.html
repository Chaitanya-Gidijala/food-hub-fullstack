Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Go to your Dashboard and click on "Active Orders". You will see real-time status updates from the restaurant preparation to the delivery partner arriving at your doorstep.

What are your delivery hours?

Our platform is active 24/7, but exact delivery availability depends on the operating hours of individual restaurants in your area.

How do I become a restaurant partner?

Create an account and select "Sign up as a Restaurant". You will need a valid PAN card and FSSAI license for the Admin to approve your application.
